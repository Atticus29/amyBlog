About Amy

How a california girl became a cupcake maker/feline lover

Amy was born in San Francisco, CA. She knew she had a special connection to cats at a very young age.
Even now, if you ask the neighbors what that strange meowing sound is, they are bound to answer, "It's probably Amy!"
Amy is a cupcake baker that has appeared on world renown shows like, "Kitchen Nightmares,"COPS,"and "How to Catch a Predator."
She loves spending time at the local animal shelter, often spending hours singing to the kittens. Here we can observe her in her natural habitat.
